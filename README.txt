This website is of University of Victoria.
In this website, first you visit the home page, and from the home page you can navigate in member page, registration page and contact us page.
In registration page you have to add your information and then if all the information is correct then it should be printed on the member page.
In contact us page, you can contact every department and see the emergency contact.
You can also add new students from phpmyadmin using database.
This website has all the necessary page for the university registration and information.
